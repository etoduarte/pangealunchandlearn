## Meeting: Pangea Marketing Suite Section Rework

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Participants:** Cal (facilitator), user

### Key Insights
- The current carousel (Problem → Idea → Solution) was an assumption that ran ahead of the user's vision
- The real story follows ONE creative asset through three phases of visibility
- Facebook's reporting structure fragments creative performance across campaign line items (audience, geo, etc.)

### Decisions Made
- **Replace carousel with scroll-driven animation** — Rationale: scroll IS the story, no clicks needed, fits site's storybook pattern. Confidence: high.
- **Three phases, one continuous scroll sequence:**
  1. **The Problem** — Facebook Ads Manager mock table, many rows with placeholder thumbnails, one hero creative with real art (stands out visually)
  2. **The Hack** — Rows collapse/morph into Excel pivot table, hero creative consolidated into one line with combined metrics
  3. **The AI-Coded Solution** — Hero thumbnail lifts off and becomes a squircle on Signal Map, top-right Star quadrant, other squircles appear around it
- **Labels**: "The Problem", "The Hack", "The AI-Coded Solution" (not "The Idea" / "The Solution")
- **Signal Map is the AHA moment** — hero visible among all other creatives, instantly scored
- **Hero creative**: "Send Money Fast — Video A" — Star category, $18.5K spend, 820K impressions
